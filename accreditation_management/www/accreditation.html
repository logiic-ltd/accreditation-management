About Accreditation

Accreditation is a voluntary process that provides assurance about the quality of education offered by institutions. It helps maintain standards and supports continuous improvement, benefiting both the institution and its students.

Benefits of Accreditation:

  • Enhanced reputation and credibility
  • Improved quality of education
  • Access to funding and resources
  • Recognition of qualifications
