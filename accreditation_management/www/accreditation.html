About Accreditation

Accreditation is a voluntary process that provides assurance about the quality of education offered by institutions. It helps maintain standards and supports continuous improvement.

Benefits of Accreditation:

  • Enhanced reputation and credibility
  • Improved quality of education
  • Access to funding and resources
  • Recognition of qualifications
